About

Thanks for stopping by!

I'm a patient, tenacious, 30-something looking to start a career in web development. I mostly enjoy working in JavaScript, and can work with HTML and CSS as well. Recently I've been learning MongoDB, as I love working with data. I'm completely self-taught, which means I'm good at searching for and implementing the answers I need, and am always looking to learn and grow as a programmer and an individual.

In my personal life, I'm a hobbyist photographer, a cat owner, and an aunt to some awesome nieces and nephews. I enjoy a good pun, delicious coffee, most any kind of chocolate, and singer/songwriters. I also like planning and tracking things, whether it's inventory and work orders at the office, or my own habits, pull requests, shipping emails, and goals.
