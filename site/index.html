Who I Am

Hi! I'm Linda. If you work with me for any amount of time, you'll learn three things about me:

  • I'm resilient. The process of learning, applying, and improving is a regular cycle in my work. Frustration can hold me back for a time, but I always keep at it until I accomplish what I'm trying to do.
  • I'm perseptive. It's relatively easy for me to pick up on where others are coming from, be it figuring out what a customer's trying to explain, or knowing when my co-workers need to vent so everyone can move forward. While I don't enjoy them (and prefer to avoid them), navigating tense situations and striving to understand where people are coming from comes naturally to me.
  • I'm analytical. I love organizing and digging into details. I'll catch proof reading mistakes that others miss. Tracking jobs, maintaining spreadsheets, and puzzling out discrepancies and bugs is where I really shine. Pulling together information from multiple sources and compiling them all into one resource brings such a sense of accomplishment and joy.
One of my favorite parts of my job is getting to bring ideas to life. No matter the scale, being able to see something take shape and go out into the world is such a joy.

My Goals
